#  IndiSafe — AI Tourist Guardian

##  Demo Tourist Safety Project

IndiSafe is an AI-powered predictive safety system designed to protect tourists in real time which can be used Internationally.

---
##Abstract

Traveling should be about discovering new places, cultures, and experiences — not worrying about personal safety. Yet millions of tourists face risks every year, from scams and theft to getting lost in unfamiliar environments where help may not be immediately available. IndiSafe is designed as a proactive digital guardian that stays with travelers at every step of their journey. Instead of reacting after something goes wrong, it continuously analyzes location context, travel conditions, and potential risks to warn users before danger escalates. With features like live GPS tracking, real-time risk scoring, emergency SOS alerts, nearby service detection, and an AI travel assistant, IndiSafe brings multiple safety tools into one unified platform. Our goal is to empower tourists — especially solo travelers and vulnerable groups — to explore confidently, knowing that intelligent protection is always by their side. By making safety accessible, real-time, and preventive, IndiSafe aims to transform tourism into a worry-free experience for everyone
